title: Have been selected as a 2025 PyTorch Ambassador
---

I’m thrilled to share that I have been selected as a PyTorch Ambassador for the 2025–2026 cohort. This program, hosted by The Linux Foundation and the PyTorch Foundation, brings together a global community of experts dedicated to advancing the ecosystem of open-source deep learning.

As a PyTorch Ambassador, I look forward to contributing to the community by sharing insights on high-performance computing, distributed training, and the latest advancements in generative AI. It is an honor to help foster the growth of the PyTorch framework and support researchers and engineers in building the next generation of intelligent systems.

More details about the program and the current ambassadors can be found here:

[https://pytorch.org/programs/ambassadors/](https://pytorch.org/programs/ambassadors/) 

I am excited to engage with this vibrant community and to help drive innovation within the PyTorch ecosystem over the coming year.
